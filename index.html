is a genetic algorithms template library written in C++. It's a port (only the G.A. parts) of Spiral to C++.

It provides a base (aggregated by many different classes) to build upon, depending your specific needs.

This means, that almost with the same effort you can develop a simple or a complex genetic algorithm.

Finally, a couple of examples are available in your disposal for your convinience.

Enjoy your GeneticSoup!
